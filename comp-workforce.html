Computers in the Workforce

Uses

Computers can be used in many ways in the workforce, one way is as robots such as in factories like car factories where much of the work is done by machines. Computers may also be used in automated decision making see our automated decision making page.

Benefits

Here are the main benefits of using computers in the work force:

  • Unpaid labour
  • Machines can work 24/7
  • No health and saftey concerns
  • Opens up more tech based jobs maintaining and improving the machines

Concerns

The main concern is replacing cheap labour however ultimatly it means these people can spend their time either becoming further educated and then go into more high skilled positions.
