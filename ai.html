Artificial Intelligence

Is it likely that AI will replace us?

This is very fair question to ask and the answer is almost certainly will. However, we must not be scared of it and we must not reject it. Take the calculator for example, it put many people out of a job however is this really a problem because it never replaced the job of the mathematician it enhanced their capabilities. However people are scared because their lively hood is on the line, their job, their profession. What we need is a world where people don't worry about their financial situtation and people can just work because they enjoy doing it and care for the people around them not because if they don't they can't survive.
